Property Records

Property records are essential documents that provide information about ownership, transfers, mortgages, and other vital details related to real estate assets. These records are crucial for various stakeholders such as property owners, buyers, sellers, government agencies, and legal entities. Digitalizing property records involves converting physical documents into digital formats, which offers several benefits including improved accessibility, enhanced data security, and streamlined processes.

Image Processing: Once scanned, the digital images may undergo image processing techniques to enhance readability, adjust contrast, remove noise, and ensure optimal quality.


Optical Character Recognition (OCR): OCR technology is employed to extract text from the scanned images. This enables the conversion of image-based text into machine-readable and searchable formats.


Data Entry and Indexing: Extracted text and relevant metadata from the property records are entered into a database. This includes details such as property addresses, owner names, parcel numbers, legal descriptions, transaction dates, and any other pertinent information.
