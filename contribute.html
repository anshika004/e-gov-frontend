How to Contribute to Kaagaz Kavach

We greatly appreciate your interest in contributing to Kaagaz Kavach. There are several ways you can help us improve and grow:

  1. Spread the Word: Tell your friends, family, and colleagues about Kaagaz Kavach. Share our website and social media pages to help us reach more people.
  2. Provide Feedback: Share your thoughts, suggestions, and feedback with us. Your input helps us understand how we can better serve our users.
  3. Report Bugs: If you encounter any bugs, errors, or issues while using Kaagaz Kavach, please let us know. We rely on user feedback to identify and fix problems.
  4. Contribute Code: Are you a developer? Consider contributing to Kaagaz Kavach's open-source codebase on GitHub. We welcome contributions of all sizes, from bug fixes to new features.
  5. Translate: Help us make Kaagaz Kavach accessible to users around the world by translating our website and documentation into different languages.
  6. Donate: If you find Kaagaz Kavach useful and would like to support our ongoing development efforts, consider making a donation. Your contributions help cover hosting costs, development tools, and other expenses.

If you're interested in contributing in any way or have questions about how you can help, please contact us. We appreciate your support!
